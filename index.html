Our style

Plein De Vie was founded in 2017 by Tania Rodriguez and Diana Duran. We both have a love for beauty and style that eventually led us to the Aveda Institute in 2013 where we first met.

We‘re primarily known for our color work with balayage, color melt, ombre, and highlights. But we also deliver a range of other services such as extensions, waxing, makeup, special event styling, and Brazilian blowouts.

Our salon may be small, but we always make sure all our clients feel welcomed and are well taken care of.

We are open from 9am to 7pm, Monday through Saturday. Please see our stylists‘ booking pages for specific availability.
